ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing 2021

The annual ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing (SoCC'21) brings together researchers, developers, users, and practitioners interested in cloud computing. ACM SoCC'21 is the 12th edition of the premier conference on cloud computing; it is the only conference co-sponsored by the ACM Special Interest Groups on Management of Data (SIGMOD) and on Operating Systems (SIGOPS). SoCC'21 will take place virtually, and may have an in-person (hybrid) component in Seattle, Washington if health guidelines permit. As in prior SoCC conferences, SoCC'21 will foster connections between the academic and industrial communities operating in the cloud space.
